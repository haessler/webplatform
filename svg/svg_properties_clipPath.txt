===Remarks===
An IRI is similar to a URL. For example, an SVG file called &lt;code>myDrawing.svg&lt;/code> located at &lt;code>http://contoso.com&lt;/code> might have the following IRI: &lt;code>http://contoso.com/myDrawing.svg&lt;/code>.
