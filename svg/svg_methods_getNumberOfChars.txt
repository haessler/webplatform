===Remarks===
The total number of characters  includes referenced characters from &lt;code>tref&lt;/code> reference, regardless of whether they  are  rendered.
The total number of characters  is  effectively equivalent to the length of the [[dom/properties/textContent|'''textContent''']] attribute from the [http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid{{=}}203747 DOM Level 3 Core] specification (section 1.4), if that attribute also expands &lt;code>tref&lt;/code>  elements.
|Import_Notes=
