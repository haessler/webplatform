===Remarks===

The '''x2'''  property represents a ''relative'' coordinate for lowercase [[svg/elements/path|'''path''']] commands (for example, '''q'''  and '''c'''), and an ''absolute'' coordinate for uppercase '''path''' commands (for example, '''Q''' and '''C'''.)
