===Remarks===
The '''x2'''  property represents a ''relative'' coordinate for lowercase [[svg/elements/path|'''path''']] commands (for example, &lt;code>q&lt;/code>  and &lt;code>c&lt;/code>), and an ''absolute'' coordinate for uppercase '''path''' commands (for example, &lt;code>Q&lt;/code> and &lt;code>C&lt;/code>.)
